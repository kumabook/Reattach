Your terminal, on your phone.
Control your tmux sessions even when you're away from your PC.

If you're using AI coding assistants like Claude Code, you can respond to prompts right from your phone with push notifications.

【Features】
• View and control tmux sessions
• Send commands from keyboard
• Switch between multiple sessions and panes
• One-tap quick commands for frequent actions

【Remote Access】
Access securely from anywhere using VPN or Cloudflare Tunnel.

【Requirements】
• macOS or Linux
• tmux
• reattachd (free, open source)

Setup guide: https://github.com/kumabook/Reattach
