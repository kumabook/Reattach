Features

tmux Session Management

View and control your tmux sessions from your iPhone or iPad. All your sessions are listed with their windows and panes. Tap to connect and start working remotely.

Command I/O

See your terminal output in real-time. Monitor long-running tasks, watch build progress, or check on your AI coding assistant. Send commands directly from your phone.

Command History

Save frequently used commands for quick access. No need to type the same commands over and over. One tap to send any saved command to your terminal.

Push Notifications

Get notified when your AI coding assistant needs input or when tasks complete. Never miss an important prompt ‚Äî respond from anywhere without returning to your desk.

Easy Setup
