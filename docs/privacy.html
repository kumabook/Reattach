Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Reattach ("the App") handles your information.

Data Collection

Reattach does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers operated by the developer.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Server connection settings (URL, authentication tokens)
  • Saved commands
  • Push notification device tokens

This data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties.

Network Communication

The App communicates directly with the server (reattachd) that you configure. This connection is between your device and your own server — no data passes through any intermediary servers operated by the developer.

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, Apple Push Notification service (APNs) is used to deliver notifications to your device. Your device token is stored on your configured server to enable this functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
